About the Judge

Frank Maczko was born in 1932 in Budapest, Hungry to a family of seven. Although never poor or hungry, Frank immediately understood the value of education and saw it as an escape from a life of poverty. After immigrating to Canada, Frank pursued a legal career and his journey spanned from lawyer, to secretary of the law society, to provicial court judge in his home province of British Columbia. After retiring at age 72, Frank would devote himself to his art, painting tirelessly in his new found home in Ajijic, Mexico.
